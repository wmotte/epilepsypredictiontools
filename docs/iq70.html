About this calculator

This lookup table is based on clinical nomograms from a collaborative study between Universitätsklinik OWL and the UMC Utrecht to identify predictors of postoperative intelligence quotients (IQ) and develop and validate clinically applicable prediction models two years after pediatric epilepsy surgery. The tool estimates the probability of achieving IQ ≥ 70 post-surgery, with presurgical IQ as the strongest predictor.

Reference:

Cloppenborg T, van Schooneveld MMJ, Hagemann A, Hopf JL, Kalbhenn T, Otte WM, Polster T, Bien CG, Braun KPJ. Development and validation of prediction models for developmental and intellectual outcome following pediatric epilepsy surgery. Neurology 2022; 98:e225–e235. DOI: 10.1212/WNL.0000000000013065
