About this calculator

These lookup tables are based on clinical nomograms from a collaborative study between the UMC Utrecht and multiple partners to identify predictors of JME outcome and develop and validate clinically applicable prediction models. This tool uses 9 clinical variables (1024 combinations) to estimate the probability of drug-resistant juvenile myoclonic epilepsy (JME). Drug resistance is defined as failure of two adequate anti-seizure medication trials.

Reference:

Stevelink R, Otte WM, et al. Individualised prediction of drug resistance and seizure recurrence after medication withdrawal in people with juvenile myoclonic epilepsy: a systematic review and individual participant data meta-analysis. eClinicalMedicine 2022; 53:101732. DOI: 10.1016/j.eclinm.2022.101732
