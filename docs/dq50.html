About this calculator

This lookup table is based on clinical nomograms from a collaborative study between Universitätsklinik OWL and the UMC Utrecht to identify predictors of postoperative developmental quotients (DQ) and develop and validate clinically applicable prediction models two years after pediatric epilepsy surgery. The tool applies to children with developmental delay and estimates the probability of achieving DQ ≥ 50 post-surgery, with presurgical DQ as the strongest predictor.

Reference:

Cloppenborg T, van Schooneveld MMJ, Hagemann A, Hopf JL, Kalbhenn T, Otte WM, Polster T, Bien CG, Braun KPJ. Development and validation of prediction models for developmental and intellectual outcome following pediatric epilepsy surgery. Neurology 2022; 98:e225–e235. DOI: 10.1212/WNL.0000000000013065
