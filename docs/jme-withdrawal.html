About this calculator

These lookup tables are based on clinical nomograms from a collaborative study between the UMC Utrecht and multiple partners to identify predictors of JME outcome and develop and validate clinically applicable prediction models. This tool estimates the probability of seizure recurrence after medication withdrawal in people with juvenile myoclonic epilepsy (JME) who are currently seizure-free.

Reference:

Stevelink R, Otte WM, et al. Individualised prediction of drug resistance and seizure recurrence after medication withdrawal in people with juvenile myoclonic epilepsy: a systematic review and individual participant data meta-analysis. eClinicalMedicine 2022; 53:101732. DOI: 10.1016/j.eclinm.2022.101732
